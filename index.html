Welcome to Sublime Lasercraft

Sublimation and Lasering together

Combining sublimation with lasering techniques we can create lasting memories, heartfelt gifts, & funny keepsakes

With Sublime Lasercraft, creativity meets craftsmanship. We specialize in custom sublimated and laser-cut gifts and keepsakes, designed to bring a personal touch to every occasion. Whether you need elegant home décor, one-of-a-kind mementos, or a funny keepsake. Every piece we create is made with care, precision, and love.

Based in Kendallville, Indiana, Sublime Lasercraft began as a dream shared between a daughter and her mother. With her mother's encouragement, guidance, and support, she turned her passion for crafting into a business that celebrates life’s special moments in the most meaningful way, through personalized art that lasts a lifetime.

We believe every gift should tell a story, and yours is at the heart of everything we make. Whether you’re looking for something to commemorate a milestone, decorate your home, or surprise someone you love, we’re here to bring your ideas and stories to life with custom designs that are as unique as you.
